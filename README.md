# Excel-Project
The Retail Sales Insights Dashboard provides a complete analytical view of a retail store's sales data. It highlights:

Total sales distribution by product category

Best-selling products

Sales contribution by payment method

Relationship between review ratings and sales

Sales trends across multiple years

Product category performance segmented by payment methods

The dashboard is filter-friendly and designed to support data-driven decisions for marketing, operations, and inventory planning.
